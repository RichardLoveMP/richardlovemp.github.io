Richard’s First Blog

Where there is a will, there is a way

Hello there! I am Richard Qin. This is my first blog supported by Github.

All of the blogs are based on “Markdown” format. It is interesting that I came across this format accidentally by referring to my previous blog.

From my point of view, this blog can cultivate my skills of writing and speaking. Meanwhile, it can also be a witness who can see my life as a developer.

My View of this Blog

Github-Supported: At least from my point of view, the sustainability of it is higher than some other SSH server.

DNS-Friendly: Applying for a DN (domain name) costs a lot of time for checking. However, this issue could be resolved by use 2nd-order DN under the github supported.

Noob-Acceptable: There is no doubt that every first creat their blog is based on the source on the website. As for us, there is no need to buy online SSH server. Only your computer is enough!

The Future of this Blog

Given that the lack of proficiency in “Markdown”,”Plugins”,”Unix”, I will study and put something interesting or instructive on this website.

In addition, I will also put on some of my own thoughts on daily life or things. Later I will open the message board on my blog, which we can interact more easily.
